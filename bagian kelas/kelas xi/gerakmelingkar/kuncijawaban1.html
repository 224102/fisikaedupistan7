Kunci Jawaban Latihan Soal 2

Nomor 1

Opsi A: Benar

$$v = v_0 - g t$$

$$t = \frac{v_0}{g} = \frac{5}{10} = 0.5 \, \text{s}$$

Opsi B: Benar

$$v = v_0 - g t = 5 - (10 \cdot 1) = -5 \, \text{m/s}$$

Opsi C: Benar

$$t_\text{tot} = t_\text{naik} + t_\text{turun} = \frac{2 v_0}{g} = \frac{2 \cdot 5}{10} = 1 \, \text{s}$$

Nomor 2

Pernyataan 1: Benar

$$h = v_0 t - \frac{1}{2} g t^2$$

$$v = 0 \text{ di titik tertinggi, } t = \frac{v_0}{g} = 0.5 \, \text{s}$$

$$h_\text{max} = v_0 \frac{v_0}{g} - \frac{1}{2} g \left(\frac{v_0}{g}\right)^2 = \frac{v_0^2}{2 g} = 1.25 \, \text{m}$$

Pernyataan 2: Salah

Percepatan gravitasi selalu mengarah ke bawah dengan nilai tetap \( g = 10 \, \text{m/s}^2 \)

Pernyataan 3: Benar

$$t_\text{naik} = \frac{v_0}{g} = 0.5 \, \text{s}, \quad t_\text{turun} = \frac{v_0}{g} = 0.5 \, \text{s}, \quad t_\text{tot} = 1 \, \text{s}$$

Nomor 3

Pernyataan 1: Setuju

$$h_\text{max} = \frac{v_0^2}{2 g} = \frac{5^2}{2 \cdot 10} = 1.25 \, \text{m}$$

Jika \( g \) dikurangi setengah, maka:

$$h_\text{max} = \frac{v_0^2}{2 \cdot (0.5 \cdot g)} = 2.5 \, \text{m}$$

Pernyataan 2: Setuju

Grafik kecepatan terhadap waktu: $$v = v_0 - g t$$ menghasilkan garis linear menurun.
